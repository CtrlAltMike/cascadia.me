Wildfire

Wildfire rarely arrives on the wind you can see. It arrives on the one coming over the next ridge — three days before the smoke. Here's how to read the season, harden the house, and decide when staying is the gamble.

POC notes

A warm radial glow at the top edge slowly pulses (7s, 85%–100% opacity) using --color-lantern at very low alpha. A second layer drifts a faint --color-alert hatching horizontally over 18s — reads as distant smoke-light. Both use multiply blend so the underlying tones stay correct.
