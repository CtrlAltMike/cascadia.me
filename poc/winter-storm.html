Lowlands & passes, WA/OR/BC 2024: Multi-day ice across Portland Season: December – February

Winter Storm

A Pacific Northwest winter storm rarely kills with cold alone. It kills with what fails after the cold — the power, the pipes, the road home. Here's how to be the household that's still warm on day three.

POC notes

Two layers: a 78° wind-hatch drifting horizontally (14s) and a sparse radial-gradient flake field falling on a 22s loop. Flakes use --color-cloud, hatch uses --color-stone at ~3% alpha. Sparse — closer to weather outside a window than confetti.
